Project Overview

I designed and fabricated a functional Electric Piano using scrap wood, 3D-printed components, and an Arduino microcontroller. The wooden frame and lever system provided structural support, while custom CAD-modeled keys were 3D printed for consistent geometry. Button triggers mounted beneath each key served as inputs, and sound output was generated through a piezo buzzer controlled by the Arduino.
